Frequently Asked Questions

Livongo for Diabetes, High Blood Pressure & Heart Disease

What are the benefits of the Livongo program?

Livongo makes managing chronic conditions easier. Here's how:

  • After you join, you will receive a welcome kit containing your smart device and any supplies necessary to maintain and improve your health.
  • The program has three components: ConnectedDevice, The Smart Cloud & The Care Team.

What do the three components do for me?

Data from your device is securely sent through The Smart Cloud where your readings are analyzed within seconds. You will receive a message back with relevant, actionable feedback to coach and encourage better health management.

What if my reading is "off"? What happens?

If your data is significantly out of range, a Certified Educator will reach out to you to provide real-time support, education & care.

Who is eligible ?

You must be enrolled in the BCBS medical plan and have been diagnosed with diabetes and/or high blood pressure. Your dependents (13 years of age and older) can also join if they meet the same requirements.

How much does the Livongo program cost?

Livongo is available at NO COST to you. This includes FREE supplies, coaching, and support from Certified Educators, 24/7!

What if I need additional diabetic supplies like test strips & lancets?

You can order from the Livongo mobile app or from your Blood Glucose Meter which tracks usage and prompts a simple yes/no question to re-order supplies. That's it! Supplies are then shipped right to your home.
