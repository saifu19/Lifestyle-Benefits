How livongo can help

Livongo provides FREE support and supplies for employees & their dependents enrolled in the BCBS medical plan who want help managing their chronic conditions. Livongo, along with simple changes - like being active, staying hydrated, reducing stress & getting the right amount of sleep - can have a significant impact on your health.
